Things common to some vocabularies that we cannot do at the moment

  • rdfs:Literal as rdfs:range of a property
  • using other vocab's classes as superclasses/domain/range etc. for our classes/props (e.g. bowler subclass of foaf:Agent)
  • owl:InverseFunctionalProperty, ObjectProperty, DatatypeProperty, FunctionalProperty
  • owl:DeprecatedClass, owl:DeprecatedProperty and owl:versionInfo
  • Documentation: Links to previous/latest/this version in spec
  • Documentation: classes don't have automatic insertion of "in-range-of" "in-domain-of":
  • Diagram: select a subset of classes for the overview diagram
  • Diagram: property without range or domain breaks diagram.
  • Diagram: allow more customization of diagram

What we have but isn't used by all vocabs:

  • owl:disjointWith is used by foaf and sioc, but not by skos
