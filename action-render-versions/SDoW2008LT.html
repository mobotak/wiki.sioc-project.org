SDoW2008: Ligntnight Talks

Process

As the main conference, SDoW2008 offers the opportunity to workshop attendees to present some of their work, ideas, controversy, position regarding the Social Semantic Web.

We will have about 30 minutes for the talks at the end of the workshop, and any attendee is more than welcome to present something. This is especially an opportunity for non-speakers to talk in the workshop.

Each talk will be allowed 2 minutes strict + 1 Q/A. There is no limit in the number of slides. You can even go without any of them.

If you are interested, please list your name and talk title on the following list. There is no selection process, while the number of talks will be limited by time to 10. Yet, if you come after, just add your talk on the waiting list, and we will see if time permits)
